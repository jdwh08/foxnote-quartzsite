### Definition:
Comparison of different nlp packages in Python.

---
### Notes:
**NLTK**: the original package.
**spaCy**: for production use. Info extraction, [[Named Entity Recognition]], and [[Part of Speech Tagging]].
**TextBlob**: beginner friendly. Noun phrase extraction, sentiment analysis, classification, translation, and more
**gensim**: [[Vector Space]] and [[Topic Modelling]], particularly for streaming/online data in production.
**sklearn**: #ds/ml algorithms for feature extraction, classification, clustering.
**HuggingFace Transformers**: pretrained language models for extraction, summarization, question answering, etc.
**Tesseract-OCR**: OCR method for documents.
**Regex**: rule-based string matching and extraction.
**BeautifulSoup**: parsing XML and HTML files.
**Scrapy**: webscraping and extracting structured data from webpages.
