# Definition:
A type of [[Randomized Optimization]] which uses [[Monte Carlo Simulation]] for [[Importance Sampling]].

Repeat
1. Sample from our [[Probability Distribution]].
2. Minimize the [[Cross Entropy]] between our distribution and the target distribution by changing distribution parameters.
