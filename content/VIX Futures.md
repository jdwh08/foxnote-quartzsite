### Definition:
Futures on the [[VIX Index]].
- Different expirations (monthly and weekly) which have different values.
- Cash settled
- Contract size is $\$1000 *$index

---
### Notes:
- See [[VIX Futures Term Structure]] for different expirations.
- As the future nears expiration, its price approaches the VIX index settlement price.
