### Schedule:
3x per week -- M/W/F
Alternate between A and B day workouts.

Equipment: Heavy backpack, table, chairs. Dip bars?

Do more reps over time. Once you can get 15, increase the weight or do a harder variation.

These should be done TO FAILURE unless done for time.

**A Day**
1. Alternating Lunges X3 ->(30+ reps) Jumping Lunges -> (30+) For Time
2. Push Ups X3 [back straight, don't flare elbows]
3. Table Rows x3 / Y-Raises with Backpack x3
4. Skull Crushers x3 per side
5. Single Arm Lateral Raises X3 per side
6. Leg Raises X3 per side

**B Day**
1. Two Leg Glute Bridges x3 ->() Single Leg Glute Bridges x3 [clench at the top] -> (30+) For Time.
2. Decline Push Ups x3
3. Pull Ups x3
4. Bicep Curls X3 per side
5. Crunches X3 -> Holding Crunches x3

### VO2 Max w/ kettlebell ladder
Do kettlebells swing ladders .
Start with your weight between your legs, and your feet shoulder width apart. 
Lift the weight up using your arms, and exhale during the process. Once the weight reaches near your head level, inhale and bring the weight back down to your legs. This should be a fluid swinging motion.
To find the weight needed, find a weight that you can do kettlebell swings for 10 to 15 repetitions. Afterwards, go down by 10 lb.
To train vo2 max, perform one rep while breathing as normal, and then put the weight down. Your resting time is one breath in and out, and is linked to the number of kettlebell reps that you were able to do. After doing one rep, move to doing two reps with two breaths of pause. Keep going up until you hit 20 and then go back down.
