# Definition:
Given some [[Point Estimator]] without [[Bias (estimator)]], its [[Variance (estimator)]] **cannot** be lower than the inverse of the [[Fisher Information Matrix]].

$$Var(\hat{\theta}) \ge \mathcal{I}(\theta)^{-1}$$
