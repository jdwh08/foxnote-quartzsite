# Definition:
How do we determine the "optimal" weights for each asset?

Suppose we measure risk as [[Standard Deviation]] of returns. Each asset has different risk, return, and [[Covariance]]. 

Given asset expected returns, volatility, covariance, and target return, provides the optimal weights for each asset in the portfolio.
