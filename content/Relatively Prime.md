### Definition:
Two numbers are relatively prime if the only positive number that [[Divisibility|Divides]] them is 1.
- Alternatively: _[[Greatest Common Divisor|gcd]]_$(a,b)=1$

---
### Notes:
Because of the GCD equivalency, we can test this using methods to find the GCD like [[Euclid's Algorithm]].

---
### Examples:
8 and 9 are relatively prime even if neither is prime by itself.
6 and 9 are NOT relatively prime (3).
