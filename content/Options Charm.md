### Definition:
Change in [[Options Delta]] with respect to time.

---
### Notes:
- Between -1 to +1.
- ITM calls and OTM puts have positive charm.
- OTM calls and ITM puts have negative charm.
- ATM has charm of zero.
- Options not ATM have charms accelerate towards one of the two extremes as expiration approaches.
- Charm is particularly important on the Friday-Monday rollover since the underlying doesn't trade then.
