### Job Advice:
1. Return to Meatspace: Go to local meetups, job fairs, conferences, etc. and meet with people face to face. (e.g., Meetup.com)
2. Call recruiters directly. 
	1. Avoid those spamming out job posts.
	2. Google recruiters, find some in your area, get their phone number.
	3. Tell them what you do (in plain language), which helps them know what buzzwords are.
	4. Ask them what skills are in demand and/or they are having a hard time filling out.
	5. Use the skills to build personal projects.
	6. **Let them know you're calling around, so they do not have an exclusive right to represent.**
3. Use the recruiter skills in demand to skill up.
