# Definition:
Suppose we have a sample of a bunch of [[Random Variable]]s. 
They are independent and identically distributed if...
1. They are [[Independent Events]] (do not affect each other in terms of probability)
2. They are drawn from the same #stats/distributions in their [[Data Generating Process]].


A very useful property for statistical proofs.
