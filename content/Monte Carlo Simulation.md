# Definition:
A method for estimating [[Random Variable]] based on probabilistic sampling.

Suppose we have a true simulation of the [[Data Generating Process]]. Then, we use a random number generator to repeatedly simulate this process with random inputs. By the [[Law of Large Numbers]], the sample mean is a good estimate of the true mean.

# Notes:
Your choice of sampling matters a good deal. See [[Importance Sampling]]
