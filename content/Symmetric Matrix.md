### Definition:
A matrix whose values are the same across the upper-left to bottom-right diagonal.
A matrix which is equal to its [[Matrix Transpose]]: $A^T =A$.

---
### Notes:
The opposite is a [[Anti-Symmetric Matrix]], where $A^T = -A$
