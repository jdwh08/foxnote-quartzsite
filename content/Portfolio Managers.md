# Notes:
**Types of Funds**

| Exchange Traded Fund***       | Mutual Fund                                           | Hedge Fund                         |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Buy/Sell like stocks          | Buy/Sell at EOD                                       | Must sign up an agreement. Sticky. |
| Baskets of assets are listed. | Quarterly disclosures (goals and quarterly positions) | No.                                |
| Transparent                   | Semi-Transparent                                      | No.                                |
| Small % of AUM                | Medium % of AUM                                       | % of AUM AND / OR % of Profits     |

**Investors**
- Individuals. Note HFs can only have ~100 investors.
- Institutions. E.g., University Endowments.
- Fund of Funds.

**Features**
1. Track Record (5 years for institutional)
2. Simulation / [[Backtesting]] AND story that makes sense
3. Portfolio fit (new / unique / etc.)

**Goals**
- Beat a benchmark (E.g., outperform the S&P500)
- Absolute Return (typically Long/Short)
**Metrics**
- Cumulative Return
- Volatility
- Risk/Reward ([[Sharpe Ratio]])
