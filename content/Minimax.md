# Definition:

A strategy to decide which actions to take in [[Game Theory]].

For each possible option...
1. Consider the worst case in that option (MINIMUM)
2. Pick the scenario with the best worst case (MAXIMUM)

---
# Notes:
We can get even more efficient results with [[Alpha-Beta Pruning]].
Can be solved with [[Linear Programming]].

# Examples:
Also used for decision making in Game AI.
