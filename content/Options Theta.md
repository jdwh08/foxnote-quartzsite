### Definition:
Change in price of [[Options (finance)]] with change in time / approaching expiration.

**Theta Decay** because options lose value as they approach expiration, hence decay.

---
### Notes:
- Highest theta is for at-the-money options, and lowest theta is for far OTM options.
- Theta decay becomes faster the closer the option is to expiration.
- Positive theta means you're short an option.
- Higher implied vol tends to make theta higher as well, since the extra options premium erodes more each day.
	- However, short-term changes due to volatility/[[Options Vega]] are substantially larger than due to theta.
