### Definition:
Nations Large Cap Equity Implied Volatility Index.
- First in-the-money and out-of-the-money call and put options for weekly expirations bracketing 30 days in the future.
	- SPY S&P500 ETF options, which have smaller bid/ask than SPX futures.
	- Claims that [[VIX Index|VIX]] usage of really out-of-the-money options creates noise.
- Used to calculate closed-form measure of at-the-money implied vol exactly 30 days in the future.

---
### Notes:

They also have a "TDEX" for tail risk via 3-SD deep-out-of-the-money SPY puts.
They also have a "SDEX" for skewness via put skew in SPY options.
They also have a calldex for cost of 1-SD out-of-the-money call option with 30DTE.
They also have a putdex for cost of 1-SD out-of-the-money put option with 30DTE.
They also have a riskdex measuring CallDex/PutDex.
