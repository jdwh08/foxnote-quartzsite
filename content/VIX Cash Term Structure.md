### Definition:
Other [[VIX Index|VIX]]-like indices with calculation method but at different time periods.
- VIX9D/VXST for 9 days
- VIX3M/VXV for 3 months
- VIX6M/VXMT for 6 months
- VIX1Y for 1 year

---
### Notes:
None of these indices are tradable, just calculated based on options prices.

In stable bull markets, we see a typical and stable upward sloping VIX Cash term structure. (The further out in time, the more likely a risk there will be.)
During times of stress, the term structure will flip and become downward sloping.

Shorter-term investors might care more about VIX9D/VIX ratios. Also consider percentile rank through history.
