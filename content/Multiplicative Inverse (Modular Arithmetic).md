### Definition:
A number $x^{- 1}$ so that $xx^{- 1} \equiv 1(mod\ n)$ in [[Modular Arithmetic]].
- Equivalent to the two numbers being [[Relatively Prime|Coprime]] (which ignores the mod part).

---
### Examples:
$2*3 = 1\ (mod\ 5)$

---
### Notes:
Can be found using [[Euclid's Algorithm]], [[Fermat's Little Theorem]], or other ways of finding [[Relatively Prime]] numbers.

Can be simplified and reduced using the [[Method of Repeated Squaring]].
