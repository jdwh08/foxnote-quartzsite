### Definition:
$\varphi(n) =$ the \# of integers from $\{ 1\ldots n - 1\}$ that are [[Relatively Prime]] to $n$ (i.e., _[[Greatest Common Divisor|gcd]]_$(x,n) = 1)$

---
### Examples:
-   $\varphi(12)$: 12 is relatively prime for 1, 5, 7, 11; so
    $\varphi(12) = 4$
-   $\varphi(15)$: 15 is relatively prime for 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 11, 13, 14; so
    $\ \varphi(15) = 8$
