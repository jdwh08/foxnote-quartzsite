# Definition:
A loss function for [[Regression]] tasks. AKA square of the [[Residuals]].
$l_2(y, \hat{y}) = (y-\hat{y})^2$

---
# Notes:
Penalizes large residuals more than small ones.

Empirical risk for this is equivalent to [[Mean Squared Error]]
