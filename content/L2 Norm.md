# Definition:
A [[Norm]] for [[Vector]] based around Euclidean Distance:


||\mathbf{x}||_2 := \sqrt{\sum_i^nx_i^2}$


(the square root of the sum of squares).

---
# Notes:
This is in the [[Lp Norm]] family.
We can also kinda approximate this with less computation with the $L^2$ Norm: $\sum_i^{n} x_i^2$.

### Uses
- [[Ridge Regression]], [[Ridge Regularization]]
