### Definition:
Function which maps discrete random variables to probabilities.

$p(x) = P(X=x) := P(\omega \in S : X(\omega)=x)\ \forall \ x \in \mathcal{X}$
where $\omega$ is an element in the [[Sample Space and Events|Sample Space]] $S$, and $X$ is a discrete [[Random Variable]]. 

---
### Notes:
- $p(x) \in [0,1]$
- $\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}} p(x) = 1$
