# Definition:
Using the [[Correlation (Pearson)]] for assessing ML models for [[Regression]].
1. Get $\hat{y_{test}}$. Correlate against $y_{true}$. The higher the correlation, the better.

---
# Notes:
In general, as correlation decreases [[Root Mean Squared Error|RMSE]] increases.
