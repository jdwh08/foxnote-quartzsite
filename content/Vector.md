# Definition:
An array of numbers in order.

---
# Notes:

We can also think of this as a specialized [[Matrix]] with one column. This means matrix operations do apply.

- Add and Multiply by scalar: $c x + d = \begin{bmatrix} c * x_1 + d \\ c * x_2 + d\\ \vdots \end{bmatrix}$


---
# Examples:



..\textbf{\textit{x}} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ \vdots \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 5 \\ 7 \\ \vdots \end{bmatrix}$
