# Definition:

Given some query $Q$ and some documents $D$, determine the document(s) which are relevant for the query.

---
# Notes:
- Ad Hoc when we don't know the query or documents ahead of time for preprocessing.

- Feature(s) are document parts, e.g., words in text. We can also preprocess with [[Lemmatization]].
	- Lots of words
	- Words have different meanings, meanings have different words.
