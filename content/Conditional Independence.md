# Definition:

If [[Probability]] $p(x | y, z)=p(x | z)$, then $X$ is conditionally independent of $y$ because the [[Conditional Probability]] did not matter.

An analogue of [[Independent Events]].

---
# Notes:

The assumption used for [[Naive Bayes]].
