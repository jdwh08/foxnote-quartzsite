# Definition:
Rules of probability

**Sum Rule**
- $P(X=x_i) = \sum_{j=1}^M p(X=x_i, Y=y_j)$
- I.e. the [[Marginal Probability]] is the same as the sum over the [[Joint Probability]].

**Product Rule**
- $P(x_i, y_j) = \frac{n_{ij}}{N} = \frac{n_{ij}}{c_i} * \frac{c_i}{N} = p(Y=y_j | X=x_i)*p(X=x_i)$

**Bayes Theorem**
- $P(Y|X) = \frac{P(Y)*P(X|Y)}{P(X)}$
- See [[Bayes' Rule]] for more.
