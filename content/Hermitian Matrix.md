# Definition:
A [[Matrix]] whose elements across the main diagonal are [[Complex Conjugate]].



..\begin{bmatrix}
0 & a-bi & c-di \\
a+bi & 1 & e-fi \\
c+di & e+fi & 2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$



Notice that the main diagonal is real, and the mirrors are opposite ($a+bi \rightarrow a-bi$)
