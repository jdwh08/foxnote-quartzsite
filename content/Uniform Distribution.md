# Definition:
**Equal probability spread between events in a region.**

A [[Random Variable]] $x$ has equal [[Probability]] across a range from $(a,b)$.

---
# Notes:

### Properties
- [[Probability Density Function]] is $p(x) = \frac{1}{b-a}$, or 0 outside.
- [[Cumulative Distribution Function]] is $\frac{x-a}{b-a}$, or 0/1 in extremes.
- **Mean** is $1/2 (a+b)$
- **Variance** is $1/12 (b-a)^2$
