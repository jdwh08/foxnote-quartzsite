### Definition:
The prices of [[VIX Futures]] at different months.
- Contango: normal. Upward sloping. Current prices are lower than later prices.
- Backwardation: unusual. Downward sloping. Current prices are higher than later prices. Signals markets think there is substantial short term risk.

---
### Notes:
Typically we expect VIX Futures to be in contango because longer duration creates more risk of vol spikes during that period.

As VIXF Front Month approaches the expiration date, it should approach the VIX settlement value (VRO, VIX at morning of settlement date).
