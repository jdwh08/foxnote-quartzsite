### Definition:
Change in the [[Options Delta]] with the change in the implied volatility 
- (Similar to [[Options Vega]], but for [[Options Delta]] not value).

---
### Notes:
- Measures the interaction between [[Options Delta]] and [[Options Vega]].
- Positive Vanna means option increases in price when implied vol increases AND underlying rises.
- Call options have positive Vanna, while put options have negative Vanna.
	- Increase in implied vol will increase probability of option moving in-the-money, hence affecting the delta.
