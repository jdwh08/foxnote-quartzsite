# Definition:

Essentially a [[Correlation (Pearson)]] but for categorical variables.
Formally, a standardized effect size for the [[Chi Square Test]] for independence.



..V=\sqrt{\frac{\chi^2/n}{\min(k-1, r-1)}}$



where $\chi^2$ is the [[Chi Square Test|Chi Squared]] statistic and our two random variables are from $1...r$ and $1...k$
