# Definition:
A 2-Dimensional [[Array]]. They have a shape with $m$ rows and $n$ columns.

---
# Notes:
We can also think of this as the combination of multiple [[Vector]] columns.

---
# Examples:

$$\textit{\textbf{A}}= \begin{bmatrix} a_{1,1} & a_{1,2} & ... \\ a_{2,1} & a_{2,2} & ... \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots\end{bmatrix}$$
