### Definition:
A $\sigma$-algebra of subsets of S is a collection of subsets of S with the following properties:
1. S ∈ A
2. if A ∈ $\mathcal{A}$, then A-complement $A^c$ ∈ $\mathcal{A}$
3. if $A_1, A_2, ...$ ∈ A, then $\bigcup_{i=1}^{\infty} A_i \in \mathcal{A}$
