### Definition:
Objective [[Probability]]: Used in [[Frequentist Statistics]]. 
- Proportion of times that event would occur if I were to repeat the same experiment an infinite number of times, in an independent and identical fashion

Subjective [[Probability]]: Used in [[Bayesian Statistics]].
- The probability of an event is the personal degree of belief, which may be interpreted as the personal betting odds that event will occur or not.
