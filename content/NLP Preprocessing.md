### Definition:
Methods used to alter text data before processing in NLP.

---
### Methods:

**Stop Words**
Words in a language that do not contribute meaning (and, is, are, at, has). Often removed, sometimes with punctuation.

**Stemming & Lemmatization**
Turning each word into its base: tune, tuning, tuned -> tun (stemming), or tune (lemmatization)
