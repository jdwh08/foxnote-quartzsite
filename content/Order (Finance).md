# Definition:
A request for a trade to a financial exchange.
- Buy/Sell
- Symbol
- Shares
- Order Type (Limit / Market)
	- Market: Whatever the existing price is.
	- Limit: Bare minimum acceptable price.
- Price (for Limit)

**Types of Orders done by your Brokerage**
- Not real types, done by your broker.
- STOP LOSS: once hit some low price sell
- STOP GAIN: once hit some high price sell
- TRAILING: a stop which is x%
- SHORT: sell a stock by borrowing shares from broker first
	- You short IBM, of course.
	- Broker lends shares of someone who holds IBM
	- You borrow those shares from broker.
	- You sell those shares to someone who wants to buy IBM.
	- Once holder of IBM wants shares back, you have to buy it back for them.
