# Definition:

Assume our distribution is ~ [[Normal Distribution]]. What are the tails like?
- Positive Kurtosis: Fat Tails relative to Normal.
- Negative Kurtosis: Skinny Tails relative to Normal.

---
# Notes:
[[Fat Tails]]: We see that extreme events are more likely to occur than in a normal distribution. Positive Kurtosis.

[[Skinny Tails]]: We see that extreme events are less likely to occur than in a normal distribution. Negative Kurtosis.

---
# Examples:
[[Plotting Daily Returns]]: Financial returns data tends to be "fat tailed".
- In 2009, mortgages were modeled assuming [[Independent]] and [[Normal Distribution]] of returns. This was wrong.
