# Definition:
Measure the difference between the predictions we have and the true values.

---
# Notes:
NOTE: this is different from the [[Loss Function]]:
- In [[Linear Regression]], our error function is the raw [[Residuals]].
- Our [[Loss Function]] could thus be the [[Mean Squared Error]] on the residuals.
