### Definition:
The areas in a [[Vector Space]] that can be reached by [[Linear Combination]] of the vectors.

Suppose we had vectors $v_1, v_2, v_3$. The span is any vector that can be reached by $c_1v_1+c_2v_2+c_3v_3$.
