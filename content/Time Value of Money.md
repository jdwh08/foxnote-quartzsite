# Definition:
How to discount future money to account for [[Opportunity Cost]], like [[Interest Rates]].


PV=1/(1+DR)^t$


where $DR$ is the discount rate (e.g., 10% per year) which is based on risk.

Used as part of the [[Discounted Cash Flow]] valuation.
