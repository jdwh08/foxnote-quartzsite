### Definition:
A transformation that preserves both addition and scalar multiplication. 
(for a [[Vector Space]]).

Thus, if $c_1A+c_2B=M$, then $c_1T(A)+c_2T(B)=T(M)$.

**Geometric 3B1B**: After a linear transformation, grid lines remain parallel and evenly spaced.
This allows stuff like scaling and rotating, but not stuff like curving lines.
