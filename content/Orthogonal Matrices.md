# Definition:
A [[Matrix]] which has rows and columns that are [[Orthonormal Vectors]], 

i.e., the [[Matrix Product]] against the [[Matrix Transpose]] is the [[Identity Matrix]].



..A^TA = AA^T = I$



---
# Notes:
This makes it really easy to calculate the [[Matrix Inverse]], since it's right there! $A^{-1} = A^T$.
