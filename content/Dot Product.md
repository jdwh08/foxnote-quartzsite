# Definition:
Multiply all the elements of two [[Vector]]s together, and add up the sum.



..x \cdot y = \sum_i x_i y_i$



We can also 

---
# Notes:

Also can be written with the [[Matrix Transpose]] and [[Matrix Product]], e.g., $x^T y$

### Properties
- [[Associative]]: $A \cdot (B \cdot C) = (A \cdot B) \cdot C$
- [[Commutative]] $A \cdot B = B \cdot A$.
- [[Distributive]]: $A \cdot (B+C) = A\cdot B + A \cdot C$

### Geometric Interpretation
The dot product tells us the cosine angle between two [[Vector]]s multiplied by their [[L2 Norm]]:


..x \cdot y = ||x||_2 \ ||y||_2 \cos(\theta)$


- Also used in [[Vector Projection]].
