### Definition:
Square root of the [[Variance]].  $\sqrt{E((X-\mu)^2)}$

---
### Notes:
**Properties of StDev:** Linear transformations.
Given a random variable $X$, and some [[Linear Transformation]] function $g(x) = aX + b$, 
$SD(aX+b)=|a|*SD(x)$
