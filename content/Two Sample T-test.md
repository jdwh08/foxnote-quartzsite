### Definition:
A test to see whether the population means between two samples are statistically different or not.



..t=\frac{\bar{X}_B - \bar{X}_A}{\sqrt{S_B^2/n_B + S_A^2/n_A}} \sim t_{n-2}$



---
### Notes:
To compare one sample against a value, see [[One Sample T-Test]]
