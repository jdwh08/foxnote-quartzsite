# Definition:

In [[Bayesian Statistics]], two fundamental probabilities.
- Prior Probability is our "initial estimate".
	- $p(C)$
- Then, we get some evidence (e.g., a test result)
- Posterior Probability is our "resulting estimate" after the evidence.
	- $p(C|T)$
