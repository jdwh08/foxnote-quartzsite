### Notes:
From Cappie's "How to Beat Your Addictions - Explained with Plants vs Zombies"
- You are Crazy Dave, the Self Improver. You seem crazy for doing weird self improvement things.
- You are facing Dr. Zomboss, the industries which are trying to create addictions like smoking, junk food, social media, etc.
- Your plants are healthy coping mechanisms.
- The zombies are addictions.

**Plants**
Sunflower: Creates energy used for all other plants. Sleep, good nutrition, meditation.
Peashooter: Main line of defense against addictions. Sports, #exercise, relationships, hobbies, etc.
Cherry Bomb: Your willpower. Takes a lot of energy to resist addictions and recharges slow. Avoid using this.
Walnut: Learning, knowledge, and logic against addictions (e.g., negative side effects of addictions). An effective barrier at resisting the zombies. Only stalls, needs peashooter.
Torchwood: Boosts the effects of your peashooters. Purpose, desire, passion. Gives more motivation to do your peashooter things.
Iceshroom: Mindfulness and self-control. Temporarily prevents impulsive actions by pausing them, counteracts body reflexes.
Lantern: Breaks through the fog. Remind yourself why you quit, and reinforce the idea that you need to quit.

**Zombies**
As the game progresses, zombies get stronger. Addictions also evolve and get harder to resist. Thus, we need to "upgrade" our defenses simultaneously.
